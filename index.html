Bin Jia

Wed 15 August 2018 | tags: Short Bio

I received my BS degree from Jilin University, Changchun, China in 2004 and MS degree from University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China in 2007, and the Ph.D. degree in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Mississippi State University, Starkville, USA in 2012.

I am working with Intelligent Fusion technology, inc., Germantown, MD as a project manager. Prior to IFT, I worked as a Post-Doctoral Research Scientist with the Department of Electrical Engineering, Columbia University, New York, NY.

My research interests include non-linear filtering and estimation, information fusion, time series signal processing, sensor/resource allocation, guidance and navigation, machine learning, image processing, video tracking, and high performance computing.
