Bin Jia

Dec 26, 2023 | tags: Short Bio

I received my MS degree from University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China in 2007, and the Ph.D. degree in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Mississippi State University, Starkville, USA in 2012.

I am working with Torc Robotics , an independent subsidiary of Daimler Truck, as a Staff ML engineer. Before I joined Torc, I worked with Aptiv.

My current research interests include nonlinear estimation, information fusion, object detection and tracking, and machine learning.
